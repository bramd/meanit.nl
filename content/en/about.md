Mean IT Consultancy is a consultancy focused on accessibility and software development.

## Our expertise

- **Accessibility**: We help organizations make their digital products accessible to everyone, in compliance with WCAG guidelines.
- **Development**: We build quality software with accessibility in mind from the start.

## Our approach

We believe accessibility should not be an afterthought, but an integral part of the development process.
